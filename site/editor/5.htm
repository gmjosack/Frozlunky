Frozlunky Level Editor

Useful Information

Level Design Guidelines

  • All Jungle Levels should have enough '1' tiles for a Black Market to spawn. If the game cannot spawn a Black Market on the chosen level, it will crash.
  • If your Ice Caves levels are crashing the game, move the exit to another chunk.
  • Do not require players to use Force Level, instead, go under Level->Project Settings and set the starting level to the target level.
  • Enable "Quick Restart resets to current level" under Level->Project Settings if your levels are particularly difficult to allow the player to reset to the level they died on.
  • Under Level->Project Settings set the Olmec/Yama fight level to be after your last level in the pack.
  • Do not use '=' tiles in the Ice Caves, use '+' instead.
  • Use 'm' (unbreakable) terrain tiles where you do not want the player to break through.
  • Use 'q' (anti-obstacle) terrain tiles where you do not want the game to spawn tikis / area-specific obstacles.
  • Use '.' (unmodified) terrain tiles where you do not want your terrain to be changed by the generator.
  • Use water tiles ('w') and Destroyed Cobweb entities to remove unwanted tiles/entities placed by the game.

Hell Routing

  • To allow the player to reach Hell place down a Hell Bible somewhere within reach.
    • The Hell Bible can be found either in the Entity Picker or as the '#' tile in all areas past the Mines.

Useful Tips

  • You can use Bullet entities to have high-damage entities that float in mid-air.
  • Any height water negates all fall damage (useful for falling starts).
  • Giant Spider Cobweb Projectile entity negates fall damage if the player falls through it.
  • When modifying Olmec, you can only modify the first two and last row of chunks, however you can place entities anywhere.
  • When doing Force Level on Olmec/Yama, you must enter through Quick Restart, not the lobby. Otherwise the game will put you in the end cutscene.

Working with Mouse

In Picker

  • Left Click:
    • Click Once: Pick Tile/Entity

In Tile Editor:

  • Left Click:
    • Click Once: Position cursor with one-tile size
    • Click and Drag: Position cursor and size it
  • Right Click:
    • Click Once: Position cursor and place down currently picked tile
    • Click and Drag: Draw with currently picked tile
  • Middle Click:
    • Click Once: Position cursor and erase tiles at position
    • Click and Drag: Draw with eraser

Working with Keyboard

Global

  • Arrow Keys
    • Move cursor in specified direction
  • Ctrl+Arrow Keys
    • Move cursor in specified direction (skipping 1 tile)
  • Shift+Arrow Keys
    • Size cursor in specified direction
  • Esc
    • Reset Cursor Size to top left
  • Shift+Esc
    • Reset Cursor Size to bottom right
  • Alt+Arrow Keys
    • Navigate currently picked tile/entity in tile picker
  • Ctrl+Space
    • In tile picker pick tile under cursor
  • Ctrl+P
    • Open Entity Picker (then type desired entity and press Enter)
  • Ctrl+T
    • Open Tile Picker Search (then type desired tile and press Enter)
  • Any Text Character Key A-Z a-z 0-9 !#$%&*+,-.|~
    • Place down tile by key text
  • Space
    • Erase tile and move cursor forward
  • Backspace
    • Erase tile and move cursor backward
  • Backtick (`)
    • Place down currently picked tile in tile picker
  • Ctrl+F
    • Flood fill
